How does it work:
1. Create a scene: container for everything that is being rendered
2. Choose a camera type: perspective or "old skool"
3. Choose lightning 
4. Choose a mesh: material and geometry
    - Material: color or texture
    - Geometry: shape
5. Setup renderer

Future:
